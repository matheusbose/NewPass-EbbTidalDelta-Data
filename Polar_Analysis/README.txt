# README

## Polar analysis workflow

The original Polar analysis workflow was developed by:

Pearson, S.G., Elias, E.P.L., Van Prooijen, B.C., Van der Vegt, H., Van der Spek, A.J.F., Wang, Z.B., 2022. A novel approach to mapping ebb-tidal delta morphodynamics and stratigraphy. Geomorphology 405, 108185. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.geomorph.2022.108185

The workflow was subsequently adapted and expanded by:

McPherran, K.A., de Assis Bose, M., Shawler, J.L., Sylvester, C.S., Bracero Marrero, L.I.M., 2026. The Inlets Atlas 2.0: A Database of Geomorphic Analysis Products from 13 U.S. Coastal Inlets. Manuscript under review at Scientific Data.

The version of the workflow used to generate the results in this repository includes additional modifications and adaptations by Matheus de Assis Bose for the analyses presented in the associated manuscript.
